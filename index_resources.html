Productivity

Cold Turkey

Reduce time spent on websites like Facebook or Instagram while studying and boost productivity. Blocks websites for a designated time.*Not available on Macs yet*

  • Basic: Free
  • Pro: $14.99 - One Time

Evernote

Keep organized with this web application. Evernote allows you to organize your notes into notebooks, collect web articles or handwritten notes, and allows you to talk with teamates over Evernote WorkChat. Of course Evernote allows you to sync your notes across multiple devices.

  • Basic: Free
  • Plus: $24.99/year
  • Premium: $49.99/year

Stay Focused

A Chrome Extension to help you say focused by blocking time-wasting websites. An alternative to Cold Turkey. This extension is high customizable, allowing you to block certain sites or even specific pages or in page content like images.

  • Free!

SAT Prep

Khan Acadmey

Khan Acadeny offers an in-depth, free, online course for SAT prep. They offer test prep for both the new and old SAT. They offer 4 full-length practice test and interactice practice in the form of videos, practice questions, and quizzes.

Majortests.com

This website offers free practice tests and questions. There are practice questions for each section of the SAT: Math, Critical Reading, and Writing. Majortests.com even offers a section for SAT vocabulary. You don't have to create an account either.

CollegeBoard

CollegeBoard is the official organization that makes the SAT test. Therefore their resources are the most legitimate out of every third-party test prep website. Their website offers a wealth of information on test dates, how the SAT is graded, and tons of practice questions. Make sure to make an account! That way you can see up for tests easily and see your score online after a month.



ACT Prep

The ACT

This is the official organization which makes and administers the ACT. It offers practice tests, test questions, and general ACT tips. Again make sure to make an account! An account lets you register for the ACT all from the comfort of your home.

Best ACT Prep.org

Bestactprep.org offers many ACT prep resources such as videos and articles. This website is completely free. The only catch is that users must make an account.

FreeTestPrep

Offers free practice questions and tests. Every practice quiz has a video which goes through the thought process for each question.

Scholarships

Big Future

Find scholarships, internships, etc. Offered by CollegeBoard, this service searches through more than 2,200 program.

FastWeb!

Website that finds all sorts of scholarships. Free sign up. Also gives general finanical aid and tips for scholarships.

Free-4U.com

A national database for scholarships. Must create a free account.
